Good vs Bad Buy Factors

Momentum

Recent gain should show strength without being wildly overextended. Momentum mode likes controlled moves, not exhausted pumps.

Liquidity

Volume helps entries and exits. Thin tokens can slip hard, especially when a stop loss needs to sell quickly.

Spread

The gap between bid and ask is an instant cost. Lower spread means the trade starts with less hidden loss.

Pullback

A pullback is the dip from a recent high. Small pullbacks can be okay for momentum; deeper pullbacks need recovery confirmation.

Trend

Short-term trend helps avoid buying a weak bounce. The bot currently checks whether 15m structure is rising.

Risk Controls

The paper bot uses one position at a time with take-profit, stop-loss, and time-stop exits.

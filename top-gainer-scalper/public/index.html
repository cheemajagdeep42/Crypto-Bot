# Token Signal Score Gain Pullback Spread Volume Entry Factors

Good vs bad buy factors

Momentum

24h gain should be strong, but not wildly overextended. This app starts with a 3-25% band.

Liquidity

High USDT volume and trade count matter because exits need to be easy when the signal fails.

Spread

A wide bid/ask spread is an instant hidden cost. Lower than 0.2% is the first safety filter.

Pullback

The current strategy likes a 2-5% dip from the recent 5m high, then signs of recovery.

Trend

Short-term 15m structure should still be rising. A dip in a broken trend is usually not a bargain.

Next factors

We should add RSI, candle volume spike, BTC market direction, news risk, funding, and stop distance.
